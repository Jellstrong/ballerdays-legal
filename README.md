# ballerdays-legal
Public legal documents for Baller Days, including the Privacy Policy used for App Store compliance.

This repository provides publicly accessible legal and compliance documents for the Baller Days platform, including the mobile app and web experience. These documents are hosted via GitHub Pages to meet App Store and platform transparency requirements.
Purpose
• 	Host the Privacy Policy and other legal documents required for App Store submission.
• 	Provide a stable, public URL for users to review how their data is collected, used, and protected.
• 	Keep legal content separate from the private application codebase while remaining easy to maintain.
Documents Included
• 	Privacy Policy
Outlines what information Baller Days collects, how it is used, and how users can request data removal.
Additional documents (such as Terms of Service or Data Retention Policy) may be added as the platform evolves.
About Baller Days
Baller Days is a grassroots sports platform designed to support teams, players, and communities with tools for match management, team organisation, and shared memories. This repository supports the platform by ensuring legal transparency and compliance.
