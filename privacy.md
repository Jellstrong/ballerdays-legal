Baller Days – Privacy Policy
Last updated: 20 March 2026
Baller Days (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides tools for grassroots sports teams, players, and supporters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and mobile applications.

Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information:
- Account Information — name, email address, encrypted password, and profile details.
- Team & Player Data — team membership, match data, stats, photos, posts, and other content you choose to upload.
- Usage Data — device type, operating system, app version, and interactions within the app.
- Media Uploads — photos, videos, and other media you submit to the platform.
- Cookies and Local Storage — used for login sessions and essential app functionality.
We do not collect financial information or precise GPS location.

How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
- Create and manage your Baller Days account.
- Support team management, match data, and platform features.
- Improve app performance, reliability, and user experience.
- Provide customer support and respond to enquiries.
- Maintain platform safety and prevent misuse.
We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

How We Share Information
We may share information with:
- Service Providers — such as hosting, analytics, and email delivery partners.
- Team Administrators — for features that require team management access.
- Legal Authorities — if required to comply with applicable laws.
We do not share your information with advertisers or data brokers.

Data Storage and Security
Your data is stored securely using modern encryption and access controls.
We host our services on reputable cloud providers with industry‑standard protections.
While we take reasonable steps to safeguard your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Rights
You may request:
- Access to your personal data.
- Correction of inaccurate information.
- Deletion of your account and associated data.
To make a request, contact us at jellstrong@gmail.com.

Children’s Privacy
Baller Days is designed for grassroots sports communities.
Children under 13 should only use the platform with parental or guardian consent.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate permission.
